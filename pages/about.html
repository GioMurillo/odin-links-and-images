About Page

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos and images with lots of gradients. They do not allow for transparent pixels.

GIFs

GIFs are good for simple animations. They are limited in color palette, Avoid using them for photos.

PNGs

PNGs are good for diagrams and icons. They can use transparency and don't have color palette limitations.

SVGs

SVGs are amazing. Use them whenever you can.
